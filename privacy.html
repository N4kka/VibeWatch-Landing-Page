Privacy Policy

How VibeWatch handles your data

We collect only what is needed to personalize your clip feed, deliver AI recommendations, and sync lists across devices. Below is a concise summary of the data we collect, why we use it, and how you can manage or delete it.

Last updated: January 2025 Contact: startingvibe2025@gmail.com

Key points

  • No data selling. Providers only receive what they need to run the service.
  • Manage permissions in-app and in iOS Settings (notifications, analytics).
  • Request export or deletion anytime via email; we respond promptly.
What we collect

Information necessary to run VibeWatch

Account & identity

  • Email, username, optional avatar; authentication tokens (Supabase auth).
  • Sign in with Apple details when you choose that option.

App activity

  • Clips you view, skip, like, save; lists you create; comments/reactions.
  • Search terms, AI prompts, streaming platform preferences.

Device & technical

  • Device type, OS version, language, country (for availability checks).
  • Crash logs and push notification token.

Purchases & support

  • Product identifiers, receipts, entitlement status via Apple/RevenueCat (never full card details).
  • Support messages sent to startingvibe2025@gmail.com.
How we use it

Purpose-driven processing

Run core features

  • Authenticate you, sync lists and comments, and deliver personalized clip feeds.
  • Show where titles are streaming, keep your preferences in sync, and prevent abuse.

AI recommendations

  • Prompts and minimal context are sent to our AI provider (Cerebras) to generate suggestions.
  • Requests are logged to maintain quality and prevent misuse.

Payments & access

  • Verify App Store purchases and manage Pro entitlements (Founding Member Pro or Standard Pro) with RevenueCat.
  • We rely on Apple for billing; refunds are handled by Apple.
  • We store your subscription tier information to provide appropriate feature access and maintain pricing lock for Founding Members.
  • Free and Pro users have the same data collection practices; subscription status only affects feature access, not privacy.

Quality & reliability

  • Optional analytics (Firebase when available) for performance and crash diagnostics.
  • Notifications you opt into (new clips, reminders); you can disable them anytime.
Sharing & retention

Where data lives and how long we keep it

Storage & processors

  • Supabase for user data and lists.
  • RevenueCat for entitlements; Apple for payments.
  • Firebase (when available) for analytics; TMDB/Watchmode for availability data.

Retention

  • Data is kept while you maintain an account.
  • Requests for export or deletion are honored at startingvibe2025@gmail.com.

Commitments

  • We do not sell personal data.
  • Providers only receive what’s required to deliver the service.
  • VibeWatch is intended for users 13+.
Your controls

Manage permissions and requests

Permissions

  • Notifications can be turned off in iOS Settings > Notifications.
  • Analytics (when prompted) is optional; you can opt out in settings.

Access, export, delete

  • Email startingvibe2025@gmail.com to access, export, or delete your data.
  • We’ll verify your request via your account email and respond promptly.

Changes to this policy

  • Updates are posted on this page with a new “Last updated” date.
  • Material changes may also be noted in-app.
Contact

Questions or requests?

Reach us anytime at startingvibe2025@gmail.com. Include your device, app version, and a short description so we can help quickly.
